Campus Life

School Church

Established in 1978

The church is a beautiful monument built by a famous architect. Students come here for peace and quiet. It is a getaway from their hectic life.

School Library

Made in 1980

The library is another quiet place with rows and rows containing books of all genres. Students often gather here in groups to complete their assignments and get reference from the books.

School Auditorium

Built in 1983

The school auidtorium is where meetings, seminars and shows are held. It is a place where talented students can show their skills without any fear.
